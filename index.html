Local, personal, and professional
Ellis Green Music provides elegant, engaging live jazz music, whether in a concert setting or as background music for weddings, restaurants, retirement communities, hotels, and parties.

Ellis Green is a jazz pianist/bassist from Phoenix. For nearly ten years Ellis has been performing around the valley at prestigious local venues including LON's at the Hermosa Inn, the Phoenician, and Scottsdale Fashion Square. See more below.

During his career Ellis has performed at events for clients ranging from ASU to the NFL, and for audiences including the mayors of Phoenix and Tempe and the governor of Arizona.

Ellis regularly performs in different configurations, whether as a solo pianist or as a trio with upright bass and drums— whichever is best for a client's budget/space.

Elegant and versatile
Most often a trio with piano/keyboard, upright bass, and drums, our group's instrumentation is flexible and can also include trumpet, saxophone, guitar, or even a vocalist. We strive to be lively yet tasteful, and work to manage our volume so as not to overpower conversation. This is a group well-suited for events ranging from casual get-togethers to classy black tie receptions, for venues ranging from backyards to luxury resorts.
